Biography

The Apich Organization is an open-source organization dedicated in advancing the Rust ecosystem, with a primary focus on scientific computation. Our mission is to foster a more secure and peaceful cyberspace and contributing to the Rust ecosystem. Our work is driven by three pillars: our Development and Security teams, supported by a specialized Consultancy group. We actively welcome community contributions and value the insights brought by outside collaborators.

Our flagship initiative is the RSSN-Framework, which you can explore in our GitHub repository or in our Codeberg repository. For inquiries or collaboration opportunities, feel free to reach out to us at security@apich.org.
